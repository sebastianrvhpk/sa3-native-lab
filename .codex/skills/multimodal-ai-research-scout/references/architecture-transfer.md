# Architecture Transfer Matrix

Use this matrix to convert multimodal research into notebook experiments.

| Source Pattern | Research layer / evidence utility | Translate To SA3/SAME | Likely Workbench | Likely Altitude | Evidence / Maturity Target | Caution |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Diffusion/flow inversion | SA3 flow-conditioning | Flow prompt/state scoring over `z_t`, timestep/logSNR, velocity convention | SA3 flow prompt | root rows plus `procedures/flow_scoring.py` | flow loss rows, attribution, decoded audition; selector or intervention candidate | velocity sign and timestep parameterization must be explicit |
| Attention/control maps | SA3 internal trajectory | Prompt token attribution, residual steering, or condition ablation | Residual and trajectory | root prompt rows, residual adapter, residual procedures | token loss deltas, residual probes, descriptor deltas; microscope before control | SA3 may not expose image-like spatial attention semantics |
| Video temporal coherence | SAME representation / SA3-over-SAME coupled editing | Loop lab, periodicity probes, control lanes, bridge search | SAME measurement or intervention | root looping/periodic/control lanes plus evidence | loop metrics, autocorrelation, player loop audition; selector or intervention candidate | audio time structure is multi-scale and not just frame smoothness |
| Representation probing | SAME representation / SA3 internal trajectory | Linear control probes, residual feature basis, geometry reports | SAME measurement or residual | root measurement modules | R2, heldout rows, source-preservation/novelty; microscope or selector | probe accuracy is not intervention success |
| Adapter/LoRA/finetune methods | evidence utility | Underfit external training, notebook comparison of exports | External comparison | external plus evidence/comparison procedures | descriptors, flow prompt scores, memory similarity, listening notes; comparison evidence | keep training outside this repo unless notebook consumes artifacts |
| Retrieval-augmented generation | SAME representation plus evidence utility | Latent memory index, curriculum, donor/source selection | Memory and composition | root memory/composition modules | nearest rows, geometry distance, descriptor match; selector before intervention | retrieval can accidentally reward copying |
| Preference or feedback methods | evidence utility | Promote/drop criteria, annotation-weighted selection | Ledger and promotion | evidence and ledger, not fake reward training | ledger decisions, descriptor/listening agreement; decision support | avoid fake scalar objectives without enough listening data |
